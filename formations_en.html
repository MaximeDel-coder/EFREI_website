Academic Excellence

From Bachelor to Master's degrees, EFREI trains tomorrow's digital experts through innovative and immersive pedagogy.

Project-Based Learning

At EFREI, theory is learned through practice. From the very first year, our students work on concrete projects (web development, robotics, cybersecurity) that allow them to develop their autonomy and technical know-how.

  • Cross-disciplinary projects every semester
  • Hackathons and Inter-school Challenges
  • Mentoring by industry experts
