Experience EFREI Life

More than just a school, a place to live. With over 60 associations, the campus vibrates with the rhythm of projects and student passions.

A Modern Campus

Located at the gates of Paris, the EFREI campus offers an ideal working environment: equipped laboratories, coworking spaces, a startup incubator, and relaxation areas.

  • 3 sites in Villejuif
  • Innovation Lab (3D Printers, VR)
  • Dedicated association spaces

A Rich Association Life

60+ Associations

From robotics to finance, including humanitarian aid and events, there is an association for everyone.

Major Events

Integration seminars, ski trips, galas, and conferences set the pace for the school year.

Tech Competitions

Participate in hackathons (CyberNight, Google Hash Code) and compete against the best students.

The EFREI Community

School Spirit

Mutual aid and solidarity are strong values of the school. The alumni network counts over 13,000 graduates.

Student Well-being

A listening unit and events dedicated to well-being are in place to support every student.
