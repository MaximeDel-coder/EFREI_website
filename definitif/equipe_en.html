The School of New Technologies

Since 1936, EFREI has been training generalist engineers in the digital field, major players in the digital transformation.

Who Are We?

Our Mission

EFREI's mission is to train engineers and digital experts capable of adapting to constant technological changes (AI, Big Data, Cybersecurity) while integrating ethical and managerial challenges.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Anticipating the technological future.
  • Openness: Education geared towards the world.
  • Solidarity: A strong esprit de corps within the alumni network.

Research & Labs

EFREI's research laboratory focuses on future themes such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and future networks. Our teacher-researchers regularly publish in international conferences.
